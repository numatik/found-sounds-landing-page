about

we welcome you to be a part of a free ambient concert series hosted in remote locations dear to our hearts. as asheville locals, we reminisce of a time when novel creative events were more commonplace, and we know that creativity is the beating heart of this town we call home. our hope is to revitalize these unique gathering spaces in nature. intended as a musical scavenger hunt, each gathering is somewhere in our beautiful public appalachian wilderness.

our second event will feature ambient sets by earthcry, numatik, and will franke.

follow the coordinates, and the soothing sounds and you'll find us.

more details

this is an intentional safe space. while safety will be staffed, this is an off-grid outdoor gathering in the wilderness. please be aware and come prepared. leave no trace and be respectful of others and the earth. all are welcomed. however, we have an absolutely zero tolerance policy of any sort of disrespectful or disruptive behavior, and if you make anyone uncomfortable you may be asked to leave by the organizers and security.

due to the random nature of… nature and the possibility of spontaneous location changes, we are forming a signal group chat around this community. if the weather outlook changes we can keep everyone updated.
